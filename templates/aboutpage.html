Captcha Test

The What, the Who and the How

This website is the result of a project management course at the TU Dresden.

Our idea was creating a new type of captcha test where users must draw numbers mimicking computer-written ones to verify they're not robots.

Development Team

  • Frontend: Steve Lang
  • Backend: Felix Zenker, Steve Lang
  • Project Management: Jingjing Zhang, Felix Zenker

Technical Specifications

  • Language distribution: 77.1% HTML, 17.4% Python, 4.8% JavaScript
  • AI trained on 10,000+ near-computer-style handwritten number images
  • AI calculates output based on probabilityareas of lines
  • Hosted on Replit platform
